# Sample Knowledge Base for Demo Client
# This file demonstrates the expected format for documentation

## Password Reset

To reset your password:
1. Go to Settings > Security
2. Click "Reset Password"
3. Enter your current password
4. Enter your new password twice
5. Click "Save Changes"

Note: Passwords must be at least 8 characters with one uppercase letter and one number.

---

## Account Settings

You can manage your account settings by navigating to Settings > Account.

Available options:
- Update email address
- Change display name
- Manage notification preferences
- Download account data
- View login history

---

## Billing Information

Our billing cycle runs monthly on the date you signed up.

Accepted payment methods:
- Visa
- Mastercard
- American Express
- PayPal

To update your payment method:
1. Go to Settings > Billing
2. Click "Payment Methods"
3. Add or update your card details

---

## Refund Policy

Refunds are available within 30 days of purchase for annual plans.
Monthly subscriptions are non-refundable but you can cancel anytime.

To request a refund, please contact our support team with your order number.

---

## Data Export

You can export your data at any time:
1. Go to Settings > Privacy
2. Click "Export My Data"
3. Select the data types to include
4. Click "Generate Export"

The export will be emailed to you within 24 hours.

---

## Account Deletion

To delete your account:
1. Go to Settings > Account
2. Scroll to "Danger Zone"
3. Click "Delete Account"
4. Confirm by entering your password

Warning: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be removed within 30 days.
